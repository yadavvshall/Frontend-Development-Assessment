A low ANC can indicate depletion of the bone marrow neutrophil storage pool, or that the infant will not have
enough neutrophils to fight a bacterial infection.

  • Neonates who deplete their neutrophil reserves while fighting infection are at highest risk of dying from sepsis.

      • Infants born to mothers with hypertension may have a low ANC.

      • Consider maternal history when evaluating ANC results.
